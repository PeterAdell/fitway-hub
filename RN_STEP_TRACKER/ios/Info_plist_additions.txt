<!--

  ADD THESE KEYS to your ios/{YourApp}/Info.plist:
  
  EXPLANATION:
  - NSMotionUsageDescription: Required to access motion/pedometer data (iOS 8+, displayed to user)
  - NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription: Required for GPS tracking while app is active
  - NSLocationAlwaysAndWhenInUseUsageDescription: For background location (optional, more permissions)
  - NSLocationUsageDescription: Generic location usage (iOS 11+)
-->

<string>We track your location in the background to continue recording your route when the app is not actively displayed.</string>
